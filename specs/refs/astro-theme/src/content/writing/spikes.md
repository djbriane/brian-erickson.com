---
title: On Spikes
date: 2024-11-12
summary: Why a one-day engineering spike is usually the cheapest product research you can buy.
tags: [Process, Engineering]
---

Most teams treat spikes as a planning tax. They're not — they're the highest-leverage research instrument a product team owns. A day of real code answers questions that a week of meetings can't.

The trick is to time-box ruthlessly and to throw the code away on purpose. The output of a spike is a one-page memo, not a branch.
