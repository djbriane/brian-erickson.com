---
title: The Login Screen Is the Product
date: 2023-09-21
summary: Onboarding is not a funnel to optimize. It's the first chapter, and most teams write it last.
tags: [UX, Onboarding]
---

If the first ninety seconds of your product feel like paperwork, the rest of the product has to apologize for them. The login screen is not a gate — it's the opening sentence.

Teams that treat onboarding as a content problem instead of a conversion problem tend to end up with better conversion anyway.
