## Why

Existing route-planning tools optimize for breadth — every export format, every map layer, every social feature. GPXplore optimizes for the moment after a ride or hike, when you want to look back at what you actually did, mark the good parts, and send a clean link to a friend.

## What I built

- Drag-and-drop GPX ingestion with on-device parsing (no upload required).
- Elevation, pace, and segment annotations stored in a shareable URL.
- A reading-first map view: muted basemap, the route as the figure.

## Where it is now

Public beta. Working through onboarding friction and a small set of paid features around private route libraries.
