## Context

Closely is a B2B writing tool. The bullets feature area was the most-used surface but also the most-complained-about — discovery was poor and the editor felt like a form.

## Approach

- Ran six rounds of usability tests against a click-through prototype before any engineering work.
- Reframed the editor around a single composition surface instead of nested fields.
- Partnered with the platform team on a backend migration that unblocked a long-blocked sync feature.

## Outcome

Weekly active use of the bullets surface climbed 38% over two quarters. Support tickets in the area dropped by roughly half.
